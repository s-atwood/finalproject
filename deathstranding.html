Death Stranding

When Hideo Kojima is involved, you know it won't be an ordinary gaming experience. Death Stranding is a beautiful game. It was impossible not to stop and photograph the stunning Icelandic inspired landscapes. The environment is vast and often lonely. I aimed to highlight the beauty, uniqueness, and solitude of Death Stranding with my shots.

Click to play for an enhanced experience and enjoy audio from the game!

I am very proud of this photo. This pose was achieved by having Sam jump through the air and opening photomode to pose him mid air. The pose is a nod to the character Higgs.

My favorite photo from Death Stranding. I love the post apocalyptic ruins surrounding Sam while the sun streams into the void. Sam feels so small against the nothingness surrounding him. The orange glow of BB on Sam's chest feels comforting.

I wanted to represent Sam's grief and isolation with this shot. Though Sam's isolation is a chosen path, it's still painful for him. (And I could never pass up a fantastic framing opportunity!)

Death Stranding is a weird game (in the best way). I love the surrealism elements in the game. The design often feels like a strange and beautiful dream.

This is an unintentional collaborative photo between myself and another player. Death Stranding allows you to see what other players have built in their games. I climbed a mountain and came across another players sign suggesting I take a walk. I found a little sky bridge nearby and walked through the clouds.
