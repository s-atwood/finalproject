Control ▼

A wonderfully abstract gaming experience. Control has my favorite environmental design of any game. The game's design encourages creative virtual photography outside of the box and maybe...this reality.

For an enhanced experience, enjoy audio from the game!

The lights in this room reminded me of jellyfish. I liked the idea of Jesse swimming through the scene as if she was underwater.

Inspired by the painting "The Son of Man" by surrealist artist René Magritte.

This was in a hidden area of the Mail Room. I loved the way the ceiling faded away into nothing.

I rotated this picture 180 degrees so it looked like Jesse was gently falling. I added a glitch effect to add an element of surrealism.

Inspired by the painting "Not to Be Reproduced" by surrealist artist René Magritte.
