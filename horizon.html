Horizon: Forbidden West

Horizon: Forbidden West's landscapes have a lovely softness much like a painting. Every inch of the bright and colorful environment invites the player to stop and admire the view. So I did.

Click to play for an enhanced experience and enjoy audio from the game!

I was inspired by concept art for this shot. The light streaming through the trees, beautiful greenery covering the forest floor, and Aloy resting feels serene.

Leaning into the sci-fi elements of the game, this shot looks other-worldly and is intended to peak the viewers interest.

I wanted a unique take on a portrait. Aloy's spear conceals her eyes and adds a mysterious element to the shot.

This photo shows the failed technology of "The Old Ones". They flew too close to the sun and nearly destroyed the planet. I find it interesting that the planet ultimately survived and "The Old Ones" are nothing more than memory.

This shot was inspired by concept art and was intended to look like a HFW trading card. I love Aloy's confident pose as it is true to character.
