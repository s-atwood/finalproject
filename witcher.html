The Witcher 3

The Witcher 3 is a massive open-world game. There are populous cities, bogs, mountains, and rocky seas. I felt the most inspired to photograph the game in moments of solitude when the wind was howling. The life of a witcher is ultimately a lonely path and I chose to lean into that.

Click to play for an enhanced experience and enjoy audio from the game!

The lantern Geralt is holding is an item you receive early in the game. It has has little use beyond a few missions, but I love the green glow of it. I find myself constantly whipping out the lantern to add a little interest to my photos.

Climbing up a mountain the player has zero need to climb, I found a beautiful and clear night sky. I added the clouds in ProCreate to give the illusion Geralt was parting the sky with his lantern. I also played around with the depth of field to guide your eye to Geralt.

My favorite shot from The Witcher 3. With this shot I wanted to showcase the solitude of a Witcher's life. Geralt stands alone in a field, but the photo isn't sad. Geralt finds peace in the quiet.

I'm currently playing and shooting The Witcher 3. As I progress, I will have more photos available.
