Stray 🐾

The environmental design of Stray is outstanding. The delapitated and cramped buildings, inspired by the real life city of Kowloon, beg you to explore. The largely abandonded city still feels so alive with the thoughtful design details that unveil the cities past. Though Stray lacks a photo mode, I found a way to capture its beauty.

Click to play for an enhanced experience and enjoy audio from the game!

Inspired by Hong Kong street photography, I loved the motion in this photo.

A little clever maneuvering and I was able to capture this birds eye view of a robot on a stroll. Added noise and desaturation gives the photo a more realistic feel.

This photo has many layers to it. The longer you view it the more details reveal themselves to you, bringing more life to the photo.

My favorite shot from Stray. I had my character perched high above the street to get this shot. Reducing the contrast in this image gave it a more drawn feel and is intended to be reminiscent of anime.

Not unique to this photo, I was inspired by Hong Kong street photography. I added a layer of noise to the photo for a little grit.
