Ghost of Tsushima

Ghost of Tsushima is a stunning work of art. A love letter to Japan, the environmental design is breathtaking, vibrant, and fresh. My virtual photography for GoT took inspiration from many sources thanks to the expansive photomode.

Click to play for an enhanced experience and enjoy audio from the game!

The shadows cast on Jin's face reminded me of war paint. A little reduction in the contrast gives this shot a more painted look.

I took inspiration from old landscape paintings when framing this shot. Every flower, leaf, and breeze feels like a brush stroke.

This shot took some clever posing. I had to time a roll and blink to give the illusion of Jin taking a little nap amongst the flowers.

A foggy morning and some sun rays creates a beautiful blue backdrop that works well against the black silhouette of Jin.

This shot was inspired by watercolor paintings. I reduced the contrast to give a more drawn feel and to give it a Studio Ghibli feel.
